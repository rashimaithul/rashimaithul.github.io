About Me

Figure: Erasmus Scholars Meet (2019) at EU Office, New Delhi, India

I am currently a recipient of the prestigious European Union funded Erasmus Mundus Joint Master Degree in Tourism Development and Culture (TourDC), since 2020.

As a part of this degree program, I have already completed my first year at University of Glasgow & University of Malta and currently pursuing my final semester at the ISCTE - University Institute of Lisbon, Portugal.

Before Erasmus, I was a full time tourism marketing professional with over 3 years of international experience in developing and implementing marketing strategies for high-achieving online and offline campaigns pertaining to travel & tourism. I possess excellent commercial awareness, market insights with expertise in Marketing Management & Communications, Market Research and Strategic thinking to drive profitable businesses.

I hold expertise in developing marketing strategies and collateral with minimal budgets - including brochures, PowerPoint presentations and social media content. For the same I have delivered a lot of sales pitches on sustainable tourism to international audiences.

Apart from this I also hold strong understanding of the global tourism industries; keen interest in responsible tourism/sustainable tourism; understanding of the needs of a wide range of stakeholders including governments, industry bodies, local business owners, media and academics

In my past work experiences I was able to manage events and training programs domestically for over 100+ delegates, developing theme and contents, run sheets and event documentation, producing marketing collateral, coordinating logistics (including venues, travel and visas), corresponding with presenters and attendees and managing event teams.

In terms of soft skills I am an individual who is Creative, Proactive, Results-Oriented, with Problem Solving, and Excellent Communication Skills. I possess rigid attention to details and have the ability to work under pressure. Possesses a sharp eye for detail, a hands-on approach to work and a dedication to excellence. Highly adaptable, able to juggle multiple projects and enjoys working with diverse groups of people and being challenged.

Besides my academics & research activities, I am actively involved with the Erasmus Mundus Assciation Enactus Aston(Social Entrepreneurship Society) as a Market Research Manager, where my main task includes Managing promotion, positioning, creating & employing innovative market strategies for Enactus Aston's future endeavors.
