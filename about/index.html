About Me

Hello, My name is Rashi Maithul. Thank you for stopping by my website. Currently, I am a student of Erasmus Mundus Joint Master Degree in Tourism Development and Culture (TourDC) with a fully funded scholarship sponsored by the European Union, starting 2020.

As a part of this degree program, I have already completed my first year at University of Glasgow & University of Malta and am currently pursuing my final semester at the ISCTE - University Institute of Lisbon, Portugal.

Before Erasmus, I was a full time tourism marketing professional with over 3 years of international experience in developing and implementing marketing strategies for high-achieving online and offline campaigns pertaining to travel & tourism. I possess excellent commercial awareness, market insights with expertise in Marketing Management & Communications, Market Research and Strategic thinking to drive profitable businesses.

Besides my academics & research activities, I am actively involved with the Erasmus Mundus Association as a Social Media Manager for Project Support Unit. It has been an enriching experience working with an international team of volunteers who are motivated to promote education, multicultural exchange, diversity and inclusion of cultures, networking and new project opportunities for Erasmus Mundus students and alumni.

Apart from this, I work with Green Destinations - as an Intern. Green Destination is a non-profit organisation working towards developing a sustainable tourism industry.
