About Me

Figure: Erasmus Scholars Meet (2019) at EU Office, New Delhi, India

I am currently a recipient of the prestigious European Union funded Erasmus Mundus Joint Master Degree in Photonic Integrated Circuits, Sensors & Networks (PIXNET), since 2019. As a part of this degree program, I had already completed his first two semesters in TeCIP Institute, Scuola Superiore Sant'Anna, Pisa, Italy, and currently attending my third semester at Aston Institute of Photonic Technologies, Aston University, Birmingham, United Kingdom, before going to Osaka University, Japan in the fourth semester to complete my master's thesis. I received my undergrad with degree - Integrated B.Tech (Electronics & Communication Engineering) + M.Tech (Wireless Communication & Networks) from the School of ICT, Gautam Buddha University in May 2018. Where apart from my regular academic projects I had done various internships related to embedded systems, telecommunication & hardware design.

 

My experience encompasses working on various in lab optical system design software tools & workbench along with expertise in modeling, design, & simulation of advanced photonic integrated circuits. I have also published several novel research papers in reputed Science Cited Indexed (SCI) journals such as Elsevier, Springer, including an international conference paper in the concference of Optical Society of America (OSA), LAOP-2018 in Peru.

 

My primary research interests mainly includes topics related to Photonics, Integrated Optics (Photonic Integrated Circuits), Advanced & Hybrid Optical Communication Systems, Free Space Optics, Spatial Division Multiplexing, Ultradense Wavelength Division Multiplexing. Other research interest includes Machine Learning, Big Data, Embedded Systems, IoT.

 

Technical Expertise :

  • Photonic CAD tools - OptiSuite, Optisim, COMSOL, Lumerical ( FDTD, MODE, DEVICE, INTERCONNECT )
  • Layout Design Tools - KLayout, Mentor Graphics L-Edit, HSPICE
  • Scientific softwares - MATLAB, ns2/3, QualNet
  • EDA Tools - OrCAD, Multisim, LTSpice, EAGLE
  • Programming languages - Python, R, C/C++, TCL/TK, PHP, mySQL
  • Frameworks - Git, Hadoop, CodeIgniter, Laravel , LATEX
 

In the first year of my Erasmus Mundus Joint Master Degree at Scuola Superiore Sant'Anna, Pisa, Italy, I have also worked on edX course UBCx: Phot1xSilicon Photonics Design, Fabrication, and Data Analysis by Prof. Dr. Lukas Chrostowski. Where, I learn to design, model, and characterize different silicon photonics circuits including micro-ring resonators, power splitters, Bragg-grating filters, electro-optic modulators, and label-free biosensors, etc through Lumerical Suite, KLayout & Mentor Graphics L-Edit.

 

Besides my academics & research activities, I am active with Enactus Aston(Social Entrepreneurship Society) as a Market Research Manager, where my main task includes Managing promotion, positioning, creating & employing innovative market strategies for Enactus Aston's future endeavors.
